Senior Design

The Aerial Land Inspection System (ALIS) is a solution to remotely and autonomously map

the terrain of a potential work site from the air by taking several aerial photos and later

stitching them into a Google Maps-like program. This document introduces the proposed design

of ALIS developed by the second development team.
